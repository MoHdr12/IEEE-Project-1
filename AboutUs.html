Happy Concept

Being Happy/Embodying Happy-ness!

We are a discount store that offers great savings continuously, no matter the season. We differ from traditional retail shops on the fact that the focus in on saving and prices.

How do we stay happy?

Happy is owned by Gray MacKenzie Retail Lebanon, a large holding group that owns Spinneys supermarkets and Grab'NGo convenient stores. Being part of Gray MacKenzie holding allows us to benefit from its enormous purchasing power. Thanks to this, HAPPY can offer a wide range of quality exclusively imported products, mainly from Europe, at competitive prices in addition to local well know brands at the best prices. We have also extended our support to the local market through the Happy private label that covers a large range of food and non-food products from local and international suppliers.
